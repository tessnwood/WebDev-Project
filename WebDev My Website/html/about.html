Born and raised in Salt Lake City, I'm a fan of the mountains, all four seasons, and being involved with the community.

I've been studying at BYU for the past few years and am genuinely curious about everything. After struggling to pick a major my first few years at BYU, I finally found Experience Design and Management which allowed me learn frameworks for solving tricky problems in any field.

The time I've spent in teaching, training, and supervising roles showed me that I love helping others learn effectively. There's nothing like facilitating an "a-ha" moment for someone. For now, I'm chasing those moments in any context!
